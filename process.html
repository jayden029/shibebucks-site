How It All Works

Behind every powerful meme coin is a powerful process—and at ShibeBucks, we believe in transparency, community, and simplicity.

It all started with a fair launch. No pre-mine. No VC deals. No whales. We dropped ShibeBucks straight into the hands of the people who matter most: YOU.

At launch, we burned 20% of the total supply, reducing overall circulation and creating instant scarcity. This burn wasn’t just symbolic—it was a signal: we’re here for real value.

Next, we allocated:

  • 26% to a liquidity pool for easy and stable trading
  • 24% to community rewards and airdrops
  • 20% to marketing and awareness
  • 10% to development

All funds are visible and traceable. We’re building with full transparency. Every move, every allocation, and every major decision goes through the community DAO. Why? Because decentralization isn’t just a buzzword—it’s the way forward.

Our roadmap is broken into phases, each carefully planned to maximize momentum and excitement. From staking and gaming, to NFT integrations and real-world collabs—we’re building something bigger than just a token. We’re building a platform where fun and finance collide.

The ShibeBucks process is simple: be transparent, empower the community, and keep the vibes high. That's how we win. That’s how you win.
